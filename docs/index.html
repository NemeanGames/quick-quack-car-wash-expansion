Quick Quack Car Wash Analytics Portfolio

Welcome to the analytics portfolio for Quick Quack Car Wash. This project demonstrates a range of business analytics techniques applied to sample data about car‑wash competitors, membership revenue, customer sentiment and growth trends. Explore the interactive dashboard or read the accompanying report to learn more about market insights and expansion strategy.

About This Project

The expansion of Quick Quack Car Wash into new markets requires understanding the competitive landscape, forecasting future growth and evaluating membership profitability. The datasets provided include competitor counts by state, growth trends for major car‑wash brands, membership revenues, forecasted store openings and a small sample of customer reviews. By combining geospatial, time‑series and sentiment analyses, we can paint a picture of opportunities and risks for future expansion.

Use the navigation above to view the interactive dashboard, which contains an embedded map and charts summarising the data. The report (generated from R Markdown) dives deeper into the methodology and interpretation of results.

This site is part of a business analytics portfolio. See the GitHub repository for source code and data.
